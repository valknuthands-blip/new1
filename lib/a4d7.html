About Me

Vivash Singh is a Fiji-based Multimedia Designer, Content Creator, and Web Developer who builds digital experiences that command attention.

His career has taken him inside the creative operations of globally recognised organisations — including Mitsubishi Motors, Coca-Cola, and Apple-affiliated businesses — where he contributed across branding, marketing visuals, and multimedia production at a professional level.

His work sits at the intersection of art and strategy: visually refined, intentionally crafted, and built to perform. From colour-graded campaigns to responsive digital platforms, every project is driven by a singular standard — world-class quality, rooted in authenticity.

Recognised by international creative communities for his mastery of photomanipulation and visual compositing, Vivash brings a rare combination of technical precision and artistic vision to everything he creates.

Creating from the Pacific. Recognised globally.
